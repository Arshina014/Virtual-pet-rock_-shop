Adoption Information

Welcome to the Virtual Pet Rock Zoo! We're thrilled you're considering adopting a virtual pet rock. This page provides all the information you need to bring a new rocky friend into your life.

Adoption Process

Adopting a virtual pet rock is easy and fun! Follow these simple steps:

1. Choose Your Rock

Browse our selection of unique virtual pet rocks, each with its own personality and story.

2. Fill Out the Adoption Form

Complete the adoption form with your details and preferences.

3. Pay the Adoption Fee

A small adoption fee helps us maintain the zoo and care for our virtual rocks.

4. Receive Your Adoption Package

Once your adoption is complete, you'll receive a certificate and care guide.

Adoption Fees

The adoption fee for a virtual pet rock is ₹10000. This fee covers the cost of maintaining our virtual zoo, providing care for the rocks, and ensuring a smooth adoption process. Your support helps us continue to offer this unique experience to rock lovers everywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a virtual pet rock?

A virtual pet rock is a digital pet with a fun personality and a backstory. It lives happily in our virtual zoo and brings joy to your digital life!

How do I care for my virtual pet rock?

Your rock will come with a care guide, but it mostly enjoys love, attention, and occasional compliments. No feeding required!

Can I adopt more than one rock?

Absolutely! You can adopt as many rocks as you like. Each one has a unique personality waiting to be discovered.
